How far we go

Depth isn’t a longer interview. It’s a different instrument at each level.

0 fm
What people will tell you

Claimed behaviour, stated preference, ranked reasons. It is real data and it is the cheapest to collect, which is exactly why so much research ends here.

Where most studies stop
60 fm
What they mean by it

Laddering and projective interviewing — the same ground approached from four directions until the answer stops being tidy. The untidy version is the useful one.

140 fm
What they actually do

Observation in context, plus behavioural data where it exists. Stated and actual behaviour diverge in every category we have worked in; the size of that gap is a finding on its own.

240 fm
Why the two disagree

The floor is the reconciliation, and it only holds if it explains all three layers above it. An explanation that fits one layer is a quote. One that fits all of them is something you can build on.
