How the map was built

Eleven candidate dimensions. Two survived. A dimension earns a place on the axis only if moving along it changes who wins — not because the data for it happens to be lying around. Price tier and share of voice are easy to plot and explain nothing here: every brand in the category sits inside the same band. Each candidate is tested against purchase behaviour before the map is drawn, and the ones that only sort the category tidily are thrown out.
