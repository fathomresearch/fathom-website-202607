Our point of view

Data isn’t just proof, it’s permission.

Most research treats numbers as evidence — something to point to after a decision’s already been made, in case it needs defending. We treat them as what makes the decision possible in the first place.

A statistically sound read isn’t a formality. It’s what lets you walk into the room and commit — not hedge, not caveat, commit. We don’t round up, and we don’t cherry-pick the cut that tells the story you wanted. The number has to earn the recommendation. That’s the whole discipline.
